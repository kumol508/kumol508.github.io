Unmasking Africa

Expanding the Outlook on African Art and Design

Through programming and workshops, the Unmasking Africa initiative aims to promote a greater appreciation for contemporary African art and design. Attendees are able to learn about contemporary African artists and create their own works. These intimate workshops provide a space open to discussion and offer a memorable experience.

Stay tuned for our next workshop this Spring in New York!
