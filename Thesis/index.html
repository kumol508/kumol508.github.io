Expanding the Outlook
on African Art and Design
Through workshops and lectures the Unmasking Africa initiative aims to promote a greater appreciation for contemporary African art and design. Attendees are able to learn about contemporary African artists and create their own works. These intimate workshops provide a space open to discussion and offer a memorable experience.
